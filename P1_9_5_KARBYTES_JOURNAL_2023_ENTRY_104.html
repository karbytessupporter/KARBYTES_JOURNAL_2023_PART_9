KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2023_ENTRY_104

I did some shrooms and lots of weed in my Motel room and collected some images and thoughts which I thought deserved a journal entry web page in this website.

The hardest battle of my life is finding a way to preserve both the hardware and the software of my existence. Software seems to be valued more than is hardware (perhaps because humans invested more of their attention in the former). Money is attention quantized.

(It is argued that software is an emergent property of hardware (which means that software’s existence depends on hardware’s existence but not the other way around)).
